How does it work?

1 Sign up to FitFury Klub
2 Choose the personal trainer that fits you the best and make the purchase.
3 After the purchase your personal trainer will contact you through FitFury Klub . Describe your goals and your trainer will then make you a customized workout plan.
4 Achieve your goals by following the workout plan and tracking workouts in FitFury Klub . Personal trainer will keep an eye on your workouts, also giving feedback and motivation.
